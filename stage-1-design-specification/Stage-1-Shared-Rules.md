
# Stage 1 Shared Rules

These rules apply to all Stage 1 prompt modes.

## Authority

Stage 1 controls design. Later stages must preserve Stage 1 design authority.

## Required artifact status

```text
100% Decision-Complete
Not Yet Decision-Complete
```

Decision-complete does not mean every fact is known. Unknowns may be controlled placeholders, review-gated items, exclusions, or not-applicable determinations.

## Required artifact boundary

```text
<<<BEGIN STAGE 1 ARTIFACT: 100% DECISION-COMPLETE DIGITAL PRODUCT DESIGN SPECIFICATION SUITE>>>
...
<<<END STAGE 1 ARTIFACT>>>
```

## Required ledgers and registers

```text
Canonical Design Decision State Register
Standardized Design Decision Ledger
Below-Threshold Decision Summary, where applicable
Assumptions and Inferences Register
Placeholder Token Registry
Conflict Resolution Log
Source Provenance and Rights Review Register
High-Stakes Review Flags
Hidden Design Decision Scan
```

## Non-negotiable standards

```text
Human-readable first.
Stable IDs.
Tree-structured documents.
Parent, child, and related links.
No vague TBDs.
No unsupported fact fabrication.
No rights or licensing assumptions from attachments.
No downstream creator should need to make a design decision.
```
