# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Participants in this project are expected to maintain a constructive, respectful, and professional environment.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Provide actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the package.
- Assume good intent where reasonable.
- Respect differences in experience level and use case.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Repeated disruptive conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Abusive, discriminatory, or threatening language.
- Deliberate attempts to weaken safety, privacy, or integrity controls.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.
